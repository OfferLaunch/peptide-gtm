## Executive Summary

We are launching two peptide e-commerce brands simultaneously to capture both ends of the market:

- **Brand 1 (Research Brand ‚Äî name TBD):** Bodybuilders, biohackers, researchers. Direct, no-BS positioning. "For research use only." Domain candidates: CorePeptides.co, CaliberPeptides.co, SovereignPeptides.co.
- **Brand 2 (VitaRevive ‚Äî vitarevive.co):** Women 30-60, moms, weight loss seekers. Warm wellness positioning. Full brand bible complete.

**Timeline:** 8-week launch ‚Üí scale through Month 6
**Revenue Target:** $50K/mo by Month 3, $150K/mo by Month 6

### Fulfillment Model: 3PL via 3PLGuys.com
- Bulk inventory ordered from supplier ‚Üí shipped to 3PLGuys FDA-registered, temperature-controlled warehouse (near LA ports)
- 3PLGuys handles pick/pack/ship to customers via their dedicated **Peptide Fulfillment** division
- Shopify integration, batch/lot tracking, compliance documentation
- Higher upfront inventory investment, but better shipping speed, QC, and multi-supplier flexibility
